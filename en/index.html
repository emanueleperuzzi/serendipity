Florence

The whole city at your fingertips

Do you want to live like a real Florentine? Between Piazza Beccaria and the true soul of Sant’Ambrogio and Santa Croce, you can discover the most authentic Florence, made of historic shops, local markets and traditional flavors.

With a pleasant walk you will reach the world icons: the majesty of the Duomo, the treasures of the Uffizi Gallery and the timeless charm of Ponte Vecchio. The entire historic center is at your fingertips.

Looking for a magical view? Follow the Lungarni and enjoy the sunset from Piazzale Michelangelo, or take a break among the nearby Florentine hills. It is an ideal location for enjoying Florence with calm, freedom and curiosity.
